Q.2) Suppose we have the same dataset as above but this time we want to get the details of students who scored more than 50 marks.

        [ {name: 'John', id: 123, marks: 98 },{name: 'Wick', id: 115, marks: 75 } ]

Q.3) Retrieve the details of students who scored more than 50 marks and have IDs greater than 120.
Ans: 

                    let ary = [];

                    for (let k = 0; k < studentRecords.length; k++){
                        if(studentRecords[k].marks > 50 && studentRecords[k].id > 120){
                            ary.push(studentRecords[k]);
                        }
                    }

                    console.log(ary);

Q.7) This time we are required to print the total marks of the students with marks greater than 50 after a grace 
                 of 15 marks has been added to those students who scored less than 50.
    
Ans: 

                        let result = [0];

                        for(let m = 0; m < studentRecords.length; m++){
                            let grace = 15 + studentRecords[m].marks;
                            if(grace > 50){
                                result[0] += studentRecords[m].marks;
                            }
                        }

                        console.log(result);
